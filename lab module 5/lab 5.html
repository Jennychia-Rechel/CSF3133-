About Universiti Malaysia Terengganu

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a university in Malaysia located in Kuala Nerus, Terengganu. It was formerly known as Universiti Malaysia Terengganu before becoming a full university.

UMT focuses on marine science, oceanography, fisheries, food technology, and environmental science. It is known for research excellence in aquatic and marine studies.
